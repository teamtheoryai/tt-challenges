## Workstream 1 — Lane density & network optimization  ·  Owner: COO (Whitman)  ·  🟢
- **Target:** +12% density / cost-per-shipment improvement. **Current: +6%, on pace.**
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) two underutilized lanes consolidated; (2) cost-per-shipment down a further 3%; (3) terminal-level dashboards live.

## Workstream 2 — TMS + telematics  ·  Owner: VP Fleet (Mehta)  ·  🟢
- **Target:** modern TMS + full telematics. **Current: ~70% of fleet live; idle-time down.**
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) telematics ≥ 90% of fleet; (2) idle-time down a further 10%; (3) driver-adoption training complete.
- **Watch:** driver adoption is the gating item (see 360).

## Workstream 3 — Tuck-in terminal acquisition  ·  Owner: CEO (Dreyer) + CFO (Ngiraingas)  ·  🟢
- **Target:** one terminal acquisition to extend the network. **Current: in diligence, on track.**
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) LOI → close; (2) financing locked (CFO); (3) 90-day integration plan ready.

## Workstream 4 — Fuel + maintenance cost-per-mile  ·  Owner: VP Fleet (Mehta)  ·  🟢
- **Target:** -8% cost-per-mile. **Current: -4%.**
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) preventive-maintenance compliance ≥ 95%; (2) fuel-card + routing program savings booked; (3) aging-fleet replacement plan aligned to capex.

## Workstream 5 — Safety / CSA → insurance  ·  Owner: VP Safety (Jennings)  ·  🟢
- **Target:** -15% insurance cost via CSA improvement. **Current: CSA improving; renewal came in below plan.**
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) CSA scores down across all BASIC categories; (2) preventable-accident rate down; (3) next renewal modeled below plan.

## Workstream 6 — Driver retention  ·  Owner: VP People (Tibbets)  ·  🟡
- **Target:** turnover 70% → 55%. **Current: 58%** — improving, not yet at target.
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) turnover < 56%; (2) driver-pay/quality-of-life program phase 2 live; (3) driver NPS up.

## Workstream 7 — CEO succession  ·  Owner: Board + CEO (Dreyer)  ·  🟡 (watch)
- **Target:** orderly, dated handoff to the COO (Whitman) within ~18 months.
- **Why it's the headline item:** ~all of the customer-relationship value sits with the founder; the EV case assumes a clean transition.
- **3–6 month indicators of success:** (1) dated succession plan + COO equity locked; (2) top-20 accounts begin transitioning to Whitman; (3) COO's own VP Ops successor in development.

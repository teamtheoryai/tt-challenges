## Cross-cutting 360 signals for the deal team
1. **The two key-person risks (CFO and CTO) are already visible in 2025** — from inside the org, not just diligence. The diff that matters is whether the firm builds bench underneath them before these become flight risks.
2. **CRO is in a year-one ramp** with a modest self-vs-rater gap; raters extend benefit of the doubt. The repeatable-revenue-engine question is deferred to 2026 — watch for hardening, not improvement.
3. **COO is the strongest leader** and carries one role; bandwidth is a forward concern, not yet a burnout warning.
4. **Delivery leadership is unstable** — Greg Talley managed out in Q4 2025; the seat enters 2026 with a new, short-tenure incumbent and low signal volume.

# Executive Hiring & Interview Debrief Notes — Vantage Managed Services

Consolidated panel notes from structured interviews for executives hired at or after the DAW acquisition. Each candidate was scored against their **target scorecard** (1–5). These notes are the primary evidence behind the **Interview Performance** baseline for hired-in executives.

> Format per candidate: panel composition · per-dimension scores · verbatim panel quotes · decision.

---

## Brad Halvorsen — CRO (interviewed 2023-09)
**Panel:** CEO (Reuben), Operating Partner, Talent DD.

| Scorecard dimension | Score | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Execution & results | 4 | Strong closer; quota president twice. |
| Strategic fit | 3 | Net-new logo motion; **recurring conversion unproven**. |
| Leadership & team | 3 | "Sells well; less evidence he builds a repeatable engine." |
| Financial acumen | 3 | Comfortable with deal economics, lighter on retention/NRR mechanics. |
| Stakeholder trust | 3.5 | Credible, polished. |
| **Weighted baseline** | **3.5** | yellow |

**Verbatim (Talent DD):** "He told the panel he 'led the turnaround of a $40M services book.' On reference, one source described it as 'Brad was on the team that did that' — he contributed, he didn't lead it. Logging a public-record discrepancy. Not disqualifying, but watch the gap between narrative and delivery."

**Decision:** Hire with a gate — comp plan must reward **recurring conversion and NRR**, not just new-logo bookings.

## Panel takeaways (deal team)

2. The **CRO** is the one hire the panel made *with a caveat*; the comp gate exists for a reason. Watch recurring conversion.
3. **VP Delivery** was a fast, lightly-evidenced hire by necessity. Treat his assessment as provisional until Q3-26.

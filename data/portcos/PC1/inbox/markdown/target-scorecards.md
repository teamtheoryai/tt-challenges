# Target Scorecards by Role — Vantage Managed Services

Role-level scorecards used to (a) score candidates at hire / org DD and (b) define the **future-state bar** each seat must clear to deliver the value-creation plan. Scale 1–5. **Target** = the score the role must reach to be "thesis-ready." **Min bar** = below this is a red flag. Weights sum to 100% per role.

> The gap between an executive's current score (see leadership assessment) and the **Target** column here is the basis for **Future State Performance** and **Actionability**.

## How to read this against performance
- **Interview Performance** (baseline) was scored against these same weights at hire / org DD.
- **Future State Performance** = projected score vs. the **Target** column as the business scales (gap to target).
- A current score **below the Min bar** on a heavily-weighted dimension is an automatic **red** and high **Actionability**.

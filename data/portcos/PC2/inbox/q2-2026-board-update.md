# Cascade Care Group — Q2 2026 Board Update

## p.1 §Executive Summary

Two material leadership developments this quarter. **First, the CFO seat is filled**:
Naomi Okonkwo joined as CFO on 2026-05-19, ending the ~5-month vacancy that followed
the prior CFO's exit. **Second, the commercial engine is showing the first real signs
of recovery** under CMO Trevor Hale after two flat quarters.

> "Filling the CFO seat removes the single biggest overhang on this business. Naomi is
> the strongest finance hire we've made across the portfolio this year — she ran FP&A
> and the lender relationship at a $400M revenue services platform and closed two
> refinancings. The lender conversations are already in better hands."
> — *M. Daugherty, Board Chair* (p.1, §Executive Summary)

The CEO question remains the open item agreed in Q1. Progress is mixed: the de-novo
growth-discipline gate slipped again, but he executed the CFO search well and has
stabilized the senior team.

## p.2 §CFO — Naomi Okonkwo (new hire)

- Start date: 2026-05-19. Tenure: ~1 month.
- Prior: SVP Finance at a $400M revenue managed-services platform; owned FP&A, treasury,
  and the senior lender relationship; led two refinancings.
- First-30-day read from the operating partner is strongly positive.

> "In three weeks Naomi rebuilt the 13-week cash model the prior CFO never delivered and
> walked the lender through it herself. The bank's posture changed in the room. This is
> a step-change for the finance function."
> — *R. Alvarez, DAW Capital Operating Partner* (p.2, §CFO)

Risk of loss: low (just hired, strong equity package). Succession: too early to assess
a backup; VP Finance role to be defined in H2.

## p.3 §CEO — succession question (open)

> "The CFO hire was the right call and the team is steadier. But the de-novo
> growth-discipline gate has now slipped three quarters running, and that is the gate the
> whole margin thesis hangs on. The board wants the CEO question — and a hard date on
> growth discipline — settled by the September meeting."
> — *M. Daugherty, Board Chair* (p.3, §CEO)

Current state: the senior team has stabilized and the CFO search closed well, but the
core VCP growth-discipline gate is still behind. The board's clear internal option is
COO Priya Nair, the ready-now CEO successor; the question is whether to commit her to
the seat or run an external process. The value-creation plan is not yet back on track.

## p.4 §Commercial — CMO Trevor Hale

> "Net-new pipeline is up 38% quarter over quarter and we closed two of the three target
> logos. After two flat quarters this is the first quarter Trevor has delivered against
> the commercial plan."
> — *Priya Nair, COO* (p.4, §Commercial)

Current state: improving. The commercial engine is no longer stalled, though one quarter
does not yet make a trend — watch Q3 for durability.

## p.5 §Net Assessment

- **CFO vacancy: resolved.** Largest risk on the prior review is materially mitigated.
- **CEO: still the watch item.** PIP continues; margin gate must land by September.
- **Commercial: early recovery.** Upgrade from "stalled," but unproven over one quarter.

# Target Scorecards by Role — Cascade Care Group

Role-level scorecards used to score candidates at hire / org DD and to define the **future-state bar** each seat must clear to deliver the value-creation plan. Scale 1–5. **Target** = thesis-ready score. **Min bar** = below this is a red flag. Weights sum to 100%.

> The gap between an executive's current score (leadership assessment) and the **Target** here drives **Future State Performance** and **Actionability**.

## How to read this against performance
- **Interview Performance** (baseline) was scored against these same weights at hire / org DD.
- **Future State Performance** = projected score vs. the **Target** column (gap to target).
- A current score **below the Min bar** on a heavily-weighted dimension is an automatic **red** and high **Actionability**. At Cascade the seats that have run closest to or below the bar in the hold are the **CMO (pipeline/growth)** — now recovering — and **VP RCM (collections/denials)**.

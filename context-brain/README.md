# context-brain/

What we know about our company, our product, and our customer — the same context a new product engineer here would get on day one. It exists so you can build for a real user instead of guessing.

**Interrogate it like a product manager.** Ask it questions through your AI tools, or read it directly: *who reads the output of this feature? what are they deciding? what would make them trust it? what does a wrong answer cost?* The build brief tells you **what** to build; this folder is how you decide **what matters** inside it. (And if the folder can't answer something — ask us. Reply to your invite email; questions are welcome and read as a good sign, not a weakness.)

*(Everything here is fictional or fictionalized — a stand-in customer world. No real company or person.)*

If you're choosing which document your brain should generate, read `02-personas.md` and `05-talent-review.md` closely — they tell you who the output is for and what it takes for that person to trust it.

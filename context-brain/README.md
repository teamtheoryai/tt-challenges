# context-brain/

What we know about our customer and our product — the same kind of context a new product engineer here would get on day one. It's here so you can make good calls: what the Second Brain is for, who uses it, what decisions it drives, and how the talent review is used. **Read whatever you need, whenever you need it.** None of it is required reading; all of it is here to help.

*(Everything in this folder is fictional — a stand-in customer world. No real company or person.)*
